Naive Vala

A simple introduction on developing elementary OS Apps

This book is in early early alpha stage

This right now is a loosely organized article series on Vala Programming for people who've never programmed in Vala. I myself have also never programmed in Vala. I have programming experience in other languages, though. My English is not perfect as well, so bare with me.

I had trouble finding a really good introduction series for beginners. The elementary guide on writing apps also assumes you having some experience with programming and I found the hurdle to get into it still too high. So I decided myself to fiddle around a little bit with Vala and elementary Apps and thought why not document my progress as well.

This page is obviously not done yet. I have to do styling and get a domain and need to figure out where this is leading. I'm planning to write this as a small book, though.
